York Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

York Chamber of Commerce - reflecting the historical and cultural significance of York.

Site Purpose

To facilitate business growth and networking in the York region.

To provide valuable resources and information to local businesses and the community.

Scenarios

1. How can local businesses benefit from joining the York Chamber of Commerce?

2. What networking events are upcoming in the York area?

3. Where can I find historical business growth data for York?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #808080 (Grey) - a professional color, mirroring the York Council website aesthetic.
Text Color: #FFFFFF (White) - ensures clear readability against the grey background.
Button Color: #FF0000 (Red) - vibrant and eye-catching, used for important call-to-action buttons.

Typography

Main Font: Grey - As it is the main Color used in the Council website.
Heading Font: Georgia - used for headings for a classic, formal look.
